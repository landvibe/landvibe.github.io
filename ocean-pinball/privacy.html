This Privacy Policy explains how Jaeseung Lee ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Ocean Pinball (the "App").

The App does not provide account registration or login features.

1) Information We Collect

A. Information collected automatically

The App may automatically collect:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, language, region)
  • Identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / AAID, app instance identifiers)
  • Usage data (e.g., in-app events such as button clicks, session duration, feature usage)
  • Diagnostics (e.g., crash reports, stack traces, performance signals)
  • Network information (e.g., IP address; approximate location derived from IP)

B. Information you provide (limited)

The App does not require you to create an account. If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you choose to share (such as your email address and message content).

C. In-app purchases

The App uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Payment information (such as credit card details) is processed by Google and is not collected or stored by us.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve features and gameplay experience (e.g., analyzing aggregated event counts)
  • Fix bugs and improve stability (e.g., crash diagnostics)
  • Serve ads and measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3) Advertising

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process identifiers (including Advertising ID), device information, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads and for measurement.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Google Ad Technology: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4) Firebase / Google Services We Use

The App uses Firebase services (provided by Google) to operate and improve the App. These services may collect and process device identifiers, app instance identifiers, usage data, and diagnostics data.

A. Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App (for example, counting button click events and other in-app events). We use this information to improve gameplay and features.

B. Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes and stability issues. Crash reports may include device state, crash stack traces, and app performance/diagnostic information.

C. Firebase Remote Config

We use Firebase Remote Config to deliver configuration values (e.g., event settings, balancing parameters, feature flags) to your app instance. Remote Config may use app instance identifiers to deliver and evaluate configurations.

D. Cloud Firestore

We use Cloud Firestore to store and retrieve app data required for features and live content. Because the App has no login, Firestore data (if any) is typically associated with an app instance rather than a named account.

What we may store in Firestore (examples):

  • Game configuration / content data (e.g., level/event data)
  • Aggregated counters or anonymous gameplay metrics (e.g., totals, balances)
  • Device/app instance scoped state needed for gameplay (if applicable)

We do not intentionally store sensitive personal information (such as real names, precise addresses, or payment card information) in Firestore.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
